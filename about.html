Our Story

Born from the sea, shaped by the aloha spirit, driven by a love of Maui's finest ingredients.

Since 2015

From Family Fishing Trips to Fine-Casual Dining

Kai Grille began with a simple memory: Chef Kaimana Akana, age seven, pulling an ono from the waters off Maalaea Bay with his grandfather. That moment — the weight of the fish, the salt in the air, the pride in his grandfather's eyes — became the foundation of everything Kai Grille is today.

After graduating from the Culinary Institute of America and staging at Michelin-starred restaurants in San Francisco and Lyon, France, Kaimana returned to Maui with a clear vision: celebrate Hawaii's extraordinary ingredients without pretension, with generosity, and with deep respect for the land and sea that provide them.

We opened our doors in Wailuku in 2015, and in the years since, we've become a destination not just for visitors, but for the families who live here — the farmers, fishermen, and neighbors who share their bounty with us every day.
