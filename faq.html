Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit to Kai Grille.

Yes, we strongly recommend reservations, especially for weekend evenings and special occasions. For Friday and Saturday dinner, we suggest booking at least one week in advance. For holidays and special events, book 2–3 weeks ahead. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability, but we cannot guarantee seating without a reservation during peak times.
Absolutely. We have several vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options on our menu. When making your reservation or upon arrival, please inform your server of any allergies or dietary needs. Our kitchen takes cross-contamination seriously, and Chef Kaimana is happy to customize dishes when possible. We work with all common allergens including shellfish, nuts, dairy, and gluten.
We describe our atmosphere as "island smart casual." Think nice aloha shirts, sundresses, or casual resort wear. We love that guests come as they are and feel comfortable — this is Maui after all. We ask that guests please wear footwear and avoid wet swimwear in the dining room. Our open-air bar area has a slightly more relaxed vibe.
Yes! We have a beautiful open-air lanai that seats 24 guests with views toward the West Maui Mountains. It's our most requested seating area and we do our best to accommodate outdoor preferences, though we cannot always guarantee it. Our indoor dining room is also open-air with trade wind circulation for a comfortable island dining experience year-round.
Our Daily Fresh Catch features whatever our local fishing partners bring in that morning — commonly ono (wahoo), mahi mahi, ahi tuna, opakapaka, or shutome (broadbill swordfish). Chef Kaimana and the kitchen team decide each morning how to prepare it based on the fish's characteristics and seasonal ingredients available. Ask your server what the catch is that evening — they always know the story behind it.
Yes! Happy Hour runs 3–6 PM daily at our bar and lanai seating. We offer $5 off all cocktails, half-price select appetizers (including our famous poke trio), and $2 off local draft beers. It's one of our favorite times at Kai Grille — a relaxed, convivial atmosphere as the afternoon light turns golden over Wailuku.
There is a public parking lot on Market Street about 100 feet from our entrance, and additional street parking is available on Market and Main Street. The lot is free evenings and weekends. We're also walkable from downtown Wailuku hotels and a short drive from the Wailuku town center.
Yes, we love hosting special occasions. Our private dining room seats up to 20 guests for intimate celebrations, rehearsal dinners, corporate dinners, or birthday events. Full restaurant buyouts are available on Monday and Tuesday evenings. Contact us at events@kaigrille.com or call (808) 555-0101 to discuss your event.
We welcome families warmly! Our keiki (children's) menu features simpler preparations of island favorites: chicken katsu strips, mild fish tacos, mac and cheese with Maui Cattle Co. beef, and fresh fruit plates. Kids' meals are $12–$14 and come with a choice of juice, milk, or coconut water.
We'd love to help celebrate! You may bring an outside cake with advance notice — just let us know when booking. We'll provide plates and candles. Alternatively, our pastry chef Leilani creates stunning custom dessert plates and celebration cakes with 48 hours' notice. Call us to discuss options — we love helping guests create memorable moments.
