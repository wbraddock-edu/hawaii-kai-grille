Our Story

Born from a Love of Maui

Kai Grille was founded in 2015 by Chef Kaimana Akana, who grew up fishing these waters with his grandfather. After training in San Francisco and Paris, he returned to Maui with a singular mission: to celebrate Hawaii's incredible ingredients with modern technique and deep aloha spirit.

Every dish on our menu has a story — from the fishermen who supply our daily catch to the upcountry farms that grow our produce. We believe food is community, and every meal at Kai Grille is an expression of that belief.
